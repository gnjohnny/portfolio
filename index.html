Category

Here are the categories of websites that I make:

Basic Websites

A basic website is a simple online platform designed to deliver essential information or services to users. It typically consists of a few core pages, such as a homepage, about page, services or products page, and a contact page.

ecommerce Websites

E-commerce websites are online platforms that enable businesses to sell products or services directly to customers over the internet. They include features like product listings, shopping carts, secure payment gateways, and user accounts for order tracking.

Web apps

Web apps are interactive websites that function like software applications, offering dynamic user experiences through a web browser without needing to download anything. They often handle complex tasks such as user authentication, data processing, and real-time updates.

Blog Websites

Platforms that focus on regularly updated content, like articles or news posts, often with user comments and social sharing features.

Landing Pages

Single-page websites focused on conversions, such as lead generation or promoting specific campaigns or products.

Educational Websites

Platforms that offer online courses, tutorials, or resources for learning, often incorporating quizzes, progress tracking, and multimedia.

Portfolio Websites

Personal or professional sites showcasing creative work, resumes, or case studies, aimed at attracting clients or employers.

Nonprofit Websites

Designed to promote causes, these sites typically include donation systems, event information, and volunteer signups.
